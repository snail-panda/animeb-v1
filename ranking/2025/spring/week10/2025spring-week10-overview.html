Spring 2025 – Week 10 Overview

June 20, 2025

Introduction

As the Spring 2025 season enters its decisive final stretch, many titles are now facing their ultimate test. Series that have carefully built up momentum through layered foreshadowing and solid storytelling are showing their strength, while others that have failed to impress so far might find it hard to recover. Week 10 often marks that final turning point—if you can’t make your move here, there may be no second chance.

Highlights and Thoughts

The unstoppable force of SHOSHIMIN: How to Become Ordinary 2 and The Apothecary Diaries continued to showcase masterful payoff of complex plotlines and carefully planted hints. In a completely different style, Catcher in the Ballpark delivered an impressive ensemble drama, weaving together the lives of players, staff, and fans into a compelling seasonal narrative. Meanwhile, Mobile Suit Gundam GQuuuuuuX kept surprising the audience with its wild, unpredictable developments—good or bad, at least it never fails to entertain.

In contrast, some once-promising series like Kowloon Generic Romance quietly fizzled out, unable to capitalize on their earlier potential.

Conclusion

As the season draws to a close, it’s time for the final episodes to leave a lasting mark. Some shows might still have that one last scene to surprise us, to etch themselves into memory—not with fireworks, but with a quiet, undeniable presence.
