Spring 2025 – Week 11 Overview

Introduction

This week marked a shift in momentum as several major titles wrapped up their arcs, while others unexpectedly reclaimed attention. It’s been a week full of emotional peaks, questionable finales, and surprising rankings.

Highlights and Thoughts

The Apothecary Diaries and The Case Files of the Ordinary dominated this week with bold storytelling and satisfying payoffs. While titles like Mobile Suit Gundam: Requiem stirred mixed reactions, they nonetheless made a strong impact.

In contrast, some once-promising series like Luronne’s Generic Romance quietly fizzled out, unable to capitalize on their earlier potential.

Conclusion

As the season draws to a close, it’s time for the final episodes to leave a lasting mark. Some shows might still have that one last scene to surprise us, to etch themselves into memory—not with fireworks, but with a quiet, undeniable presence.
