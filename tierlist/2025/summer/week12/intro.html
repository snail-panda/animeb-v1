The 2025 Summer anime season has officially come to its end, and with that, here’s the final Tier List — covering not just the shows ranked during the season, but also every title fully watched all the way through. Some series shook things up in the latter half, while others coasted along — so where did they all land in the end? Let’s dive in and find out!

< Note >

This mid-season tier list is slightly different from my usual format. Specifically, even titles that were dropped from the Weekly Ranking evaluation are included here if I have continued watching them. The purpose is to cover a wider range of shows and present their current placement from a perspective separate from the Weekly Ranking.

Please note that any series I stopped watching after placing it in the Episode 1 tier list remain marked as “Dropped” and are not included here.
Think of this tier list as a standalone overview, independent of the Weekly Ranking.
